Frequently Asked Questions

I feel lost in college. Is this normal?

Yes. Most students feel lost at some point—especially in the first 2–3 years. The problem is not confusion, it's facing it alone.

C2Club is built as a student-first peer platform where students can connect with others going through the same phase, helping you realize you're not behind—you're just early in figuring things out.

What should I actually focus on during college?

Grades matter, but skills, clarity, and peer learning matter more. Students who grow faster usually:

  • Talk to seniors
  • Learn from other students' mistakes
  • Explore opportunities early

That's why C2Club focuses on student-to-student learning instead of generic textbook advice.

Is networking really important for students?

Yes—but not fake LinkedIn networking. Real networking is:

  • Talking
  • Asking doubts
  • Helping each other

C2Club encourages organic student connections, focusing on real relationships rather than forced professionalism.
